ToDoGame

This app is a mix between a todolist and a collection game. The goal is to complete tasks to collect coins and experience levels. These coins can be used to buy items that will allow you to put creatures in your collection. This app is made in HTML/CSS/JS, the database in NodeJS with MongoDB. PS: If you go to the site, the first loading may be long due to the DB.
